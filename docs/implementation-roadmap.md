# ITSM BRD Implementation Roadmap

Source of truth: `docs/superpowers/plans/2026-04-23-itsm-brd-roadmap.md`

## Delivery Baseline

This repository already contains a broad ITSM foundation. The roadmap below makes the BRD coverage explicit, keeps the phase order stable, and separates foundation work from advanced ITSM, integrations, migration, adoption, testing, and optimization.

## Phase Order

1. Phase 0: Scope lock and governance
1. Phase 1: Foundation delivery
1. Phase 2: Advanced ITSM delivery
1. Phase 3: Integration and analytics delivery
1. Phase 4: Migration, adoption, and hardening
1. Phase 5: Optimization and CSI

## Phase Intent

- Phase 0 establishes the BRD traceability baseline, vendor/delivery governance, and repo boundaries.
- Phase 1 covers portal entry, ticket intake, requests, and knowledge search.
- Phase 2 covers change, problem, SLA, and impact-analysis depth.
- Phase 3 covers external integrations, monitoring, reporting, and dashboards.
- Phase 4 covers migration, training, UAT support, hypercare, and go-live readiness.
- Phase 5 covers automation, predictive analytics, and continuous service improvement.

## Baseline Rules

- Keep tickets, requests, changes, problems, SLA, KB, reporting, and integrations in their existing modules.
- Keep migration, testing, training, and governance as explicit workstreams rather than ad hoc tasks.
- Treat the matrix as the coverage snapshot and the roadmap as the delivery sequence.
- Update the traceability matrix whenever a BRD area moves from partial coverage toward delivery completion.
